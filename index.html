Who We are

We are a digital mentorship and micro-scholarship platform that connects high-potential students form under-resourced backgrounds with scholars, creators, and professionals who want to support them. Not just money but with time tools and community.

What we offer

Matchmaking

A smart matching tool that connects students with mentors based on shared goals and interests.
Tool Used: AI-powered interest & goal matching algorithm

Next →

MicroBoosts

A feature that lets mentors or donors fund specific student needs (graphing, calculator, a competition fee, a course subscription) with a one-time contribution.
Tool Used: Micro-donation system with verified student requests

Next →

Growth tracks

Curated resource playlists for students (engineering path, first gen college, SAT 1500+) that combine free tools, checklists and challenges.
Tool Used: Modular content system with checklists, tools, and challenges
